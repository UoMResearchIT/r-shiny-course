About

This site is for Research IT’s Introduction to Shiny course. It is based on a workshop that was given at the RSE 2018 conference workshop. It is written by David Mawdsley, with help from Joshua Woodcock and Louise Lever, who are all Research Software Engineers at the University of Manchester. Thanks to Christopher Woods at the University of Bristol for his mentoring and comments, and to Gerard Capes and Ann Gledson for beta testing the course.

This site is written using Hugo, the learn theme, and hosted on github. The course materials (i.e. the R code, data and example solutions) are hosted in a separate repository.
